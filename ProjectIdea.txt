Project Name: webmanagement system designe for a primary school.

Description:
This webmanagement system helps to manage students applications to enter the school, Studenst Registration into the 
School, and the Recording of the students Marks into the system by the school  Technical administrator.
It is designed inform of a webapplication.

How It Works:
After loging into the site the home page of the school is appears containing the application section for individuals who
seeking for admission into the school to go and fill the addmissio form.
and also contains a signin button for students that have already been registered into the system to loginto their accounts
and view their marks. Still on the home page we have some of the school futures.
once the student logsin to his account he can upload a profile picture of his choice and the view and download his marks
. 
School technical administrator:
 He is there to register the students into the system and fill in their marks for the various courses the school offers.
